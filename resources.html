A list of Peercoin resources

Mining Pools

Peercoin started as a Proof-Of-Work coin. As there are now a lot of coins generated, the network is slowly switching to Proof-Of-Stake minting. Bitcoin ASIC hardware also work on the Peercoin network. With this, the increasing popularity of Peercoin, increased minting rate, and the decreasing return on mining, competition for blocks becomes more fierce. Even with powerfull hardware it is difficult to mine alone. Mining pools combine the power of all participants to find block, and share the reward based on shares. Read the instructions of each pool carefully as connection protocols and software use can change between pools.

This is a list of pools mining PCC:
  • https://ppcoin.d7.lt/ - 1.25% fee, PPLNS: payout per share of the last N rounds
  • Coinotron - Multicoin Pool, Pay Per Share: 12% fee, Round-Based Pay Per Share: 2%
  • http://ppc.fixx.ru/ - 1% fee, PPLNS: payout per share of the last N rounds
  • https://hynodeva.com/ - Currently under upgrade

Exchanges

Exchanges are the trading websites where you can buy or sell Peercoin. Be careful not to place large sums of Peercoin on exchanges without adequate security measures as a good password and 2-factor authentication. Minting coins is also impossible on exchanges.


Buying with Fiat Money (e.g. Dollar or Euro):
  • Vault Of Satoshi - Canadian/US exchange
  • Crypto Trade - First fiat/ppc exchange
  • BTC-e - Russian exchange, largest trading volume
  • CoinMarket Exchange - US-based exchange
Buying with cryptocurrency:
  • Vircurex exchange - High volume PPC/BTC exchange
  • Cryptsy exchange - Support for almost all popular Alt-coins. Lots of trading pairs.
  • Cryptonit exchange - Low Fee exchange
  • Btc38 exchange - Chinese exchange
